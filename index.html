The JARL Youth STEAM Project aims to inspire the next generation of amateur radio operators through hands-on science, technology, engineering, arts, and mathematics education. Our four-phase approach includes:

  • P2: Youth Experience Operation Events
  • P3: School Club Support Initiative
  • P4: Mentorship Program
